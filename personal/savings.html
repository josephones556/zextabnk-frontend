Statement Savings

Entry-level and perfect for starting a savings plan. With Statement Savings you get our basic savings plan. It’s an interest bearing account1 with a little bit of freedom to start the saving process. You’ll get a free Zexta Bank Debit Card, along with the ability to transfer funds through our 24-hour phone bank, Zexta Bank Online Banking, the Zexta Bank Mobile Banking App or Zexta Bank Text Banking. By regulation, this account is limited to six pre-authorized transfers per statement cycle. However, there is no limit to in-person withdrawals.

  • Minimum Opening Balance: $25
  • Minimum Balance to Avoid Minimum Balance Fee: $100
  • Monthly Minimum Balance Fee if Balance Falls Below Minimum: $3 – Fee waived for persons 17 and under and over 60.

Money Manager

Grow your savings a little faster with tiered interest rates.

With Money Manager you get tiered interest rates1 so that money will grow even faster for you. The higher your collected balance, the more you’ll earn. Check safekeeping is included with this account.2 By regulation, this account is limited to six pre-authorized transfers per statement cycle. However, there is no limit to in-person withdrawals. You are eligible for a Bounce Protection limit of $700, which gives you peace of mind.3

  • Minimum Opening Balance: $100
  • Minimum Balance to Avoid Minimum Balance Fee: $1,000
  • Monthly Minimum Balance Fee if Balance Falls Below Minimum: $10

Premium Funds Management

Maintain a high balance, and watch that interest snowball.

With Premium Funds Management you get those tiered interest rates1 on qualifying balances so you can watch your money snowball. The higher your collected balance, the more money you’ll earn. By regulation, this account is limited to six pre-authorized transfers per statement cycle. However, there is no limit to in-person withdrawals.

  • Minimum Opening Balance: $100
  • Minimum Balance to Avoid Minimum Balance Fee: $10,000
  • Monthly Minimum Balance Fee if Balance Falls Below Minimum: $25
