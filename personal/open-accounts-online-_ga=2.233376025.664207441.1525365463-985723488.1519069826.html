A Bank In Your Corner. Literally.

We got our start in 1923 with a single office in Springfield, Missouri. We've grown a bit since then, and now you'll find us in more than 100 locations in 11 states with more than $4 billion in total assets. Over the years our focus has remained the same: understanding what really matters to our communities.
