Personal Line of Credit

Landscaping? New appliances? Laptop? Vacation? What’s on your mind?

The Zexta Bank Personal Line of Credit provides the funds you need for the ideas you have. It’s easy to access, too: online, with a check, or in person at a Zexta Banking center. Open a Personal Line of Credit for as little as $2,000 or as much as $10,000. Take the amount you need when you need it. Variable payments are based on outstanding balance.

Make it personal! Get started by applying now or at your favorite Zexta Banking center.
