Send Money

Send Money in the Mobile App allows you to show some monetary love to anyone, anywhere, for anything…and it’s virtually instantaneous! A digital person-to-person payment service, Send Money provides an alternate solution to using checks or cash. Distance and time are no longer factors when you send money to an email address or mobile number.

How Do I Get Started?

Personal accounts only; Send Money is not available for business accounts at this time.

  1. If you haven’t already, enroll in Online Banking.
  2. Log in to the Zexta Bank Mobile Banking App* (view more app features)
  3. Tap “Send Money”
  4. Enroll your debit card
  5. Enter the amount to send
  6. Verify with your debit card PIN number

*Web access is needed to use the Zexta Bank Mobile Banking App. Message and Data rates may apply, check with your wireless carrier for details. Mobile Banking is available to existing Online Banking users only. All terms applicable to Online Banking apply to mobile banking.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does Send Money work?

The transaction is processed using your Zexta Bank Debit Card. The person you send the money to will receive an email or text message letting them know you sent them money. The recipient can receive the money almost instantaneously if they accept the transfer using their debit card. If they accept the transfer using their checking or savings account and routing number, the recipient will receive the money in 1-2 business days.

Is Send Money secure?

Yes. Each time cardholders enter their PIN to complete a Send Money transaction, the PIN is encrypted so it never travels across payment channels as your actual, numerical PIN.

Do I need to register an account to use Send Money?

No enrollment is required to access Send Money. You must add your debit card to your Send Money profile to initiate payments. The recipient does not need to have an account with the Zexta Bank to receive the funds.

Is there a fee to use Send Money?

We do not assess a fee to send funds using this service. We reserve the right to update fees and will provide you with notice of such updates as required by law. The receiving institution may have limits on the number and type of Transfers allowed. The receiving financial institution may also charge a transaction fee. Please note that your mobile carrier may charge you for text messaging. Please check your mobile service agreement for details on applicable fees. We cannot guarantee and are not responsible for the availability or utility of your wireless service. You may be charged fees by your mobile carrier.

Are funds debited from my account immediately?

Yes. Send Money is similar to a one-time debit card transaction or ATM withdrawal, debiting the funds from your account immediately.

How quickly will the recipient receive the funds?

The timing depends on the recipient. The recipient can receive the money almost instantaneously if they accept the transfer using their debit card. If they accept the transfer using their checking or savings account and routing number, the recipient will receive the money in 1-2 business days.

What if my recipient does not receive their payment?

If the recipient does not claim the funds within 10 days, the funds are automatically returned to your account.

Will I receive spam text or email messages?

The only email or text messages you will receive will relate to the sending or receiving of funds by Send Money.

Will my personal information be shared with the recipient?

In order to process your transaction, basic information that you provided may be shared with the recipient. For more information about how your personal information will be used, please review our Privacy Policy.

Is this service available outside of the United States?

Send Money is currently only available to eligible transaction accounts within the United States.

How do I know this not a phishing scheme?

Look for these signs that you are in the right place to use Send Money.

  • The PIN Pad displays after entering your debit card number.
  • The PIN Pad opens in the app. There is no redirection to another site or “pop-up.”
  • The PIN Pad is branded with the Zexta Bank logo.
  • The last four digits of your card number will show on the PIN pad (preceded by 12 x’s).
What if I enter my PIN incorrectly?

If you enter your PIN incorrectly, the transaction will be cancelled and you’ll need to create the transaction again.

What if I forget my username and password?

If you forget your username or password for the Zexta Bank Mobile Banking app, please use the "Forgot Password?" option on the App or at GreatSouthernBank.com or contact Customer Service at 800-749-7113.

If you are using Cash Management, the Cash Management Administrator should call Business Support at 855-225-0115 for assistance with login information.

What if I send funds to the wrong number or email address?

Contact Customer Service at 800-749-7113.

What if I have any other issues or concerns?

Contact the Customer Service at 800-749-7113.
