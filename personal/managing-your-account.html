Helpful Tips

Monitoring transactions often and balancing your accounts every month is one of the best money management habits you can practice.

Record your transactions as often as you can in either a checkbook register or by keeping track of them through Online Banking. This will help you to keep a better eye on what your balance is and how much money you're spending.

Knowing your available balance will help prevent you from spending more money than you have available. You can double check your available balance at anytime by using our free tools including Online Banking and our 24-hour Phone Bank.

Balancing your account every month, either by using Online Banking or a checkbook ledger, can help you keep track of where you are spending your money and help make you aware of how much you have to spend or save.
