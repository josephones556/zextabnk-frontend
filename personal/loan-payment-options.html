Loan Payment Options

Automated Loan Payments

Opt for automatic withdrawal from your checking or savings account and never forget a payment. Use the Automatic Loan Payment Form in your coupon book to get started, or call the Loan Servicing Department at 888-245-2530.

Online Loan Payments

Make a loan payment through our Online Loan Payments portal.

Online Banking

Sign up for Online Banking and you can transfer funds from your Zexta Bank checking or savings account to make your loan payments.

In Person

Drop off your payment at any Zexta Banking Center .

By Phone

Set up a one-time withdrawal from a checking or savings account. A fee may be assessed for this type of transfer. Please call (800) 749-7113 Monday-Friday 8:00 a.m. - 7:00 p.m. CT, Saturday 8:00 a.m. - 6:00 p.m. CT or Sunday 9:00 a.m. - 4:00 p.m. CT to make a payment by phone.

Mail It In
