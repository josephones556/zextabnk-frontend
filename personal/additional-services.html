Safe Deposit Boxes

Your very own mini-vault is ready! Zexta Bank Safe Deposit Boxes both secure and protect your valuables.* Several size options are available (subject to availability) at most of our banking centers. Stop by your local Zexta Bank to find the best fit for you.
