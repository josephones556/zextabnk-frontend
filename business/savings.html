Statement Savings

Statement Savings is an interest bearing account1 with a little bit of freedom to start the saving process. You’ll get a free Zexta Bank Debit Card. By regulation, this account is limited to six pre-authorized transfers per statement cycle. However, there is no limit to in-person withdrawals.

  • Minimum Opening Balance: $25
  • Minimum Balance to Avoid Minimum Balance Fee: $100
  • Monthly Minimum Balance Fee if Balance Falls Below Minimum: $3

Money Manager

Grow your company savings a little faster with tiered interest rates.

With a Money Manager account from Zexta Bank, you’ll enjoy higher interest rates, tiered interest rates,1 free check safekeeping2 and a Bounce Protection limit of $700 available, which will give you peace of mind that your transactions will be paid, regardless of whether you have sufficient funds in your account or not.3 By regulation, this account is limited to six pre-authorized transfers per statement cycle. There is no limit to in-person withdrawals.

  • Minimum Opening Balance: $100
  • Minimum Balance to Avoid Minimum Balance Fee: $1,000
  • Monthly Minimum Balance Fee: $10
  • Interest Bearing: Tiered1

Premium Funds Management

Maintain a high balance, and watch that interest snowball.

Premium Funds Management offers you a great rate with a qualifying balance. You’ll also enjoy tiered interest rates1 and free check safekeeping.2 By regulation, this account is limited to six pre-authorized transfers per statement cycle. There is no limit to in-person withdrawals.

  • Minimum Opening Balance: $100
  • Minimum Balance to Avoid Minimum Balance Fee: $10,000
  • Monthly Minimum Balance Fee: $25
  • Interest Bearing: Tiered1
