Individually Owned Business

With an Individually Owned Business NOW account from Zexta Bank, you get an interest bearing checking account that allows you unlimited check-writing and cashing privileges. You enjoy no per-check charges, a free Zexta Bank Debit Card and check safekeeping. You are also eligible for a Bounce Protection limit of $700, which gives you peace of mind that your transactions will be paid, regardless of whether or not you have sufficient funds in your account, up to your Bounce Protection limit including fees.

  • Minimum Opening Balance: $100
  • Minimum Balance to Avoid Minimum Balance Fee: $500
  • Monthly Minimum Balance Fee: $10
  • Interest Bearing: Yes

Premium Business NOW

With a Premium Business NOW account from Zexta Bank you enjoy all the convenience of a regular checking account, but with tiered money market interest rates, so the higher your collected balance, the Details it will do for you. You get a free Zexta Bank Debit Card, check safekeeping included and a Bounce Protection limit of $700, which will give you peace of mind that your transactions will be paid, regardless of whether or not you have sufficient funds in your account, up to your Bounce Protection limit including fees..

  • Minimum Opening Balance: $100
  • Minimum Balance to Avoid Minimum Balance Fee: $1,000
  • Minimum Balance Fee: $7 if balance falls below minimum.
  • Interest Bearing: Tiered

Small Business Checking

The Small Business Checking account from Zexta Bank offers you a free Zexta Bank Debit Card, check safekeeping 2 , and a Bounce Protection limit of $500, which will give you peace of mind that your transactions will be paid, regardless of whether you have sufficient funds in your account or not, up to your Bounce Protection limit including fees.

  • Limited to 300 checks and/or items deposited per statement cycle.
  • Minimum Opening Balance: $25
  • Minimum Balance to Avoid Minimum Balance Fee: None
  • Minimum Balance Fee: None
  • Interest Bearing: No

Corporate Checking with Analysis

With a Corporate Checking account from Zexta Bank, you enjoy immediate access to your funds, detailed monthly statements that show all of your transactions, the ability to make night deposits with the availability of locking deposit bags, and deposit pick-up that can be arranged for larger accounts. Be sure to ask a Zexta Bank associate for details about special operating accounts to fit your needs.

  • Bounce Protection limit: $750
  • Minimum Opening Balance: $100
  • Minimum Balance to Avoid Minimum Balance Fee: N/A
  • Monthly Minimum Balance Fee: Fees assessed by Business Account Analysis
  • Interest Bearing: No

Checking Money Manager

With a Money Manager account from Zexta Bank, you’ll enjoy higher interest rates, tiered interest rates 2 , image statement at no additional cost 5 and a Bounce Protection limit of $700 available, which will give you peace of mind that your transactions will be paid, regardless of whether you have sufficient funds in your account or not 1 . By regulation, this account is limited to six pre-authorized transfers per statement cycle. There is no limit to in-person withdrawals.

  • Minimum Opening Balance: $100
  • Minimum Balance to Avoid Service Charge: $1,000
  • Monthly Service Charge: $10
  • Interest Bearing: Tiered 2

Premium Funds Management

Premium Funds Management offers you a great rate with a qualifying balance. You’ll also enjoy tiered interest rates 2 and image statement at no additional cost 5 . By regulation, this account is limited to six pre-authorized transfers per statement cycle, three of which may be by check. There is no limit to in-person withdrawals.

  • Minimum Opening Balance: $100
  • Minimum Balance to Avoid Service Charge: $10,000
  • Monthly Service Charge: $25
  • Interest Bearing: Tiered 2
