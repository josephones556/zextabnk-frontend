Positive Pay >

Pre-determined specific, actual check activity that's allowed on your business accounts.

× Positive Pay

No blindsides.

Preventing fraud is tricky. Let’s work proactively together, and on a granular level, to determine what specific, actual check activity is allowable for your business accounts...and what isn’t. We call it Positive Pay. If we detect any sneaky surprises, we’re all over it.


How does it work?

When checks or ACH transactions are presented for payment, Positive Pay matches the check / ACH information against an “ok” list previously provided by your business. Automated validation ensures each item is legitimate, or each item can be manually reviewed by either our team or yours in case an exception needs to be made.


Positive Pay provides multiple security layers and detects unauthorized items, thereby improving the reconciliation process.

Sweep Accounts >

× Sweep Accounts

Zero Balance Account (ZBA)

After deposits and payments are presented for the day, an automatic transfer initiates, ‘sweeping’ excess funds to or from a master account in order to restore all sub-accounts to a zero balance. ZBA eliminates manual internal transfers, helps prevent overdrafts, provides maximum control over balances and funding, and allows for separate accounting.
