Online Enrollment FAQ


  What information do I need to enroll?

You will need a valid account number, Social Security number (SSN), the email address that is tied to your account at Zexta Bank, and other personal information as it appears on your signature card or your most recent bank statement.

To verify your email address on file, please call Customer Service at 800-749-7113 (Monday-Friday, 8:00 a.m. – 7:00 p.m.; Saturday, 8:00 a.m. – 6:00 p.m.).

  How do I update my email or other account information?
To update your personal information on file, please call Customer Service at 800-749-7113 (Monday-Friday, 8:00 a.m. – 7:00 p.m.; Saturday, 8:00 a.m. – 6:00 p.m.), or contact your local banking center for assistance. You can find the nearest location on our locations page.
  Is there an age limit for Online Banking?
There is no age limit to view your accounts online. However, certain services, such as Bill Pay and Mobile Check Deposit, may be restricted to customers aged 18 and older.
  What kind of accounts will I have access to?

Most types of personal checking, savings, CDs, IRAs and loan accounts will show up automatically based on your account relationship.

  Can I add an account that does not show up automatically?

Once you have your Zexta Bank Online ID and password, you can request additional accounts by using the secure Mail Message Center in Online Banking, or by calling Customer Service at 800-749-7113.

  My link expired, what do I do now?
If your enrollment link expired you may enroll again to generate a new activation email. Please keep in mind that you may attempt to enroll up to three times. If you have been unsuccessful three or more times please call Customer Service at 800-749-7113 for assistance.
  How soon after I enroll in Online Banking can I enroll in Online Bill Pay?
Once your new Online Banking ID has been established, you can enroll in and begin using Online Bill Pay. For more information on Bill Pay, read our Bill Pay FAQ.
  Why was my application rejected?

There are several reasons why an enrollment may not be successful. Please make sure the following are true:

  • You are an owner on the accounts you are attempting to enroll
  • Information must be identical to what is printed on your signature card or latest bank statement
  • Date of birth, account number, and SSN may not include dashes or spaces
  • You do not already have an established Zexta Bank Online Banking ID
  • You are using the proper account number from a check or your signature card (see below)


If you have questions about your enrollment, please call Online Banking at 855-853-9403, Monday – Friday, 8:00 a.m. – 5:00 p.m. CT for assistance.
